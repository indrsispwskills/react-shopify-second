1
The First Line of Defense

Sediment Pre-Filter

Captures rust flakes, sand, pipe debris, and particulate matter before they reach your skin. Post-menopausal skin has weaker lipid cohesion — physical particles create micro-friction and barrier disruption on already-thinned tissue.

✓ Rust & sediment✓ Pipe debris✓ Micro-particles
2
The Rapid Neutralizer

Calcium Sulfite

Converts free chlorine into harmless chloride salts — instantly, even in hot water. This matters because hot showers increase chlorine volatility (you're breathing it as gas) and skin permeability. Chlorine oxidizes surface lipids, strips fatty acids, and triggers the oxidative stress that accelerates collagen breakdown.

✓ Free chlorine✓ Chlorine gas (steam)✓ Lipid oxidation
3
The Antioxidant Shield

Vitamin C — Sodium Ascorbate

Neutralizes residual disinfectants that calcium sulfite missed, including chloramines. But the real story: after estrogen declines, your body's internal antioxidant capacity drops. Vitamin C in filtration provides environmental antioxidant defense — reducing oxidative load before it contacts your skin. Applied chemistry, not wellness marketing.

✓ Chloramines✓ Oxidative stress✓ Free radicals
4
The Heavy Metal Shield

KDF-55 Copper-Zinc Media

Electrochemical redox reactions convert dissolved heavy metals (lead, mercury, nickel) into insoluble forms and suppress bacterial biofilm growth. Heavy metals catalyze oxidative stress — the same force accelerating collagen breakdown and follicular miniaturization. This stage protects both skin structural proteins and follicle integrity.

✓ Lead & mercury✓ Nickel & copper✓ Bacteria
5
The Deep Clean Finish

Catalytic Activated Carbon

Engineered with enhanced surface area to break down chloramines — which standard carbon struggles with. Also adsorbs VOCs, PFAS traces, and organic contaminants. Chloramines are more stable, harder to remove, and more persistent irritants than chlorine. This final stage ensures the lowest possible chemical load reaches your skin.

✓ Chloramines✓ VOCs & PFAS✓ Organic compounds
