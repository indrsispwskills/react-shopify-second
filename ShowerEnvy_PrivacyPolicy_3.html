What We Collect

Only what we genuinely need

We keep our data collection minimal. Here's everything we may collect and exactly why:

When you place an order:

  • Your name and email address (so we can confirm your order and reach you if there's an issue)
  • Shipping address (so your 2.0 Handheld or 5.0 Stationary arrives at your door)
  • Phone number (only if you provide it — for delivery notifications)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we never see or store your full card number)

When you visit our website:

  • Basic browsing data (pages visited, time spent — helps us improve your experience)
  • Device type and browser (so our site looks great on your phone, tablet, or computer)
  • IP address (required for security and fraud prevention)

When you contact us:

  • Your email or phone number (so we can respond)
  • The content of your message (so we can actually help)

Plain-English summary: We collect your name, email, address, and payment info when you buy something. We see basic website activity. That's it. No health data, no invasive tracking, no social media snooping.

Cookies

The digital kind (we wish they were chocolate)

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help our website work properly. Here's what we use:

Essential cookies (required): These keep your shopping cart working, remember your login, and maintain security. The site can't function without them.

Analytics cookies (optional): These help us understand how visitors use our site — like which pages are most popular or where people get stuck. We use this data in aggregate (not to identify individuals) to make the experience better for everyone.

Marketing cookies (optional): If you've opted in to marketing, these help us show you relevant content. You can disable these in your browser settings at any time.

How to control cookies: Most browsers let you block or delete cookies through their settings. Blocking essential cookies may affect your shopping experience, but analytics and marketing cookies can be disabled without any impact.

Who We Share Data With

Only trusted partners who help us serve you

We work with a small number of carefully chosen partners. They only receive the minimum information needed to do their job:

  • Stripe — Payment processing. They handle your card info under their own strict security standards. We never see your full card number.
  • Shipping carriers (USPS, UPS, FedEx) — Your name and address so your order arrives safely.

We may also share data if legally required — for example, in response to a valid court order or subpoena. If this ever happens, we'll notify you unless we're legally prohibited from doing so.

How We Protect Your Data

Serious security, explained simply

  • 256-bit SSL encryption on every page of our website — the same standard used by major banks.
  • PCI-compliant payment processing through Stripe — your card info never touches our servers.

Your Rights

Your data, your control

Regardless of where you live, we believe you deserve these rights:

  • Right to know — Ask us what data we have about you, and we'll tell you within 30 days.
  • Right to correct — If something's wrong, let us know and we'll fix it.
  • Right to delete — Want us to erase your data? Just email us. Done within 30 days.
  • Right to export — Want a copy of your data? We'll send it in a common format.
  • Right to opt out — Unsubscribe from marketing anytime with one click.
  • Right to complain — If you're unhappy with how we handle your data, contact us or your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@showerenvy.com with the subject line "Privacy Request." We'll respond within 5 business days.

For California residents (CCPA): You have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. We do not sell personal information. You may request disclosure of data collected in the prior 12 months. We will never discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.
